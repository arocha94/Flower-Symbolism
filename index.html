The Language and Symbolism of Flowers

The Sunflower symbolizes loyalty. The Marigold symbolizes creativity. This Daffodil symbolizes rebirth.
The Chrysanthemum symbolizes longevity. The Cosmos symbolizes modesty. The Zinnia symbolizes endurance.
The Red Rose symbolizes love and passion. The Hibiscus symbolizes perfect beauty. The Peony symbolizes romantic love.
The Orchid symbolizes beauty and strength. The Lily symbolizes devotion. The Tulip symbolizes friendship.
The Lavender symbolizes calmness. The Hydrangea symbolizes gratitude. The Wisteria symbolizes long life.
The Poppy symbolizes peace. The Gentian symbolizes charm and gentleness. The Morning glory symbolizes duality.
The Calla Lily symbolizes youth and purity. The White Rose symbolizes innocence. The Daisy symbolizes purity.
